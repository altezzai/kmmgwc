AMENITY CENTRE

The college has a well equipped student amenity centre to cater to the various requirements of its student community. It is quite adjacent to the main block of the institution in 1500 square feet area, where the students can get refreshed before and during intervals. The amenity centre is set in a single storey building which has many bathrooms, beds, wash basins and lots of chairs to relax during intervals and recess. This will provide all necessary first aids and students with any physical difficulty till the service of a medical practitioner is made available. Tutors of different classes will take care of the sick students along with their assigned classmates.
