IQAC-Activities

The activities of the IQAC of KMMGWC include preparation of the Annual Quality Assurance Report (AQAR) to be submitted to NAAC, based on certain quality parameters. It oversees the dissemination of information on the various quality parameters of higher education. Other activities like organization of workshops, seminars on quality related themes and promotion of quality circles are also carried out by the IQAC. The main objective of the cell is to plan and implement quality initiatives and evaluate and conducts regular meetings with quality agenda and maintains its proceedings.
