Earn While You Learn (EWYL)

The Earn While You Learn (EWYL) scheme, introduced by the Higher Education Department of the Government of Kerala, is an innovative initiative aimed at fostering a value-driven and skill-oriented educational community. It equips students with vocational skills, encourages saving habits, and promotes financial independence, bridging the gap between academic learning and real-world application. The project operates on a cyclical model, intertwining learning with work, leading to economic self-sufficiency and creating a positive financial ripple effect in society. By enabling students to discover their hidden talents and pursue activities aligned with their abilities, EWYL lays the foundation for a secure and self-reliant future. The ultimate goal of EWYL is to transform Kerala's knowledge society into a robust knowledge economy, driving holistic societal development. As a pioneering step towards addressing unemployment in a state renowned for its literacy, EWYL is a powerful tool for shaping a cohesive, progressive future.
