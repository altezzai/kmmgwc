Canteen

The canteen of the college provides all types of snacks, tea, coffee, milk and lunch to all its stakeholders. It is built in a 1600 square feet area with sufficient number of seating capacity for students and staff separately. The mid day lunch to students is provided for just Rs. 10/-, an unbelievable price that has been highly applauded by many people including the Director of Collegiate Education, who suggested that this could be implemented in other educational institutions as well. Lunch to other staff is priced at Rs. 20/- only. The canteen also provides special dishes like biriyani on all Fridays and on other special occasions. The canteen also uses unwanted wood from the campus as fuel which helps keeping the campus clean and tidy.
