Women’s Hostel

The Women’s Hostel is built inside the campus just behind the Heritage Block under the green cover of huge evergreen trees. It can accommodate a number of eighty students but due to increasing demand the number is now been extended to ninety. Students from other districts and those who are from the backward classes, have priority in admission. The hostel has its own administration with a warden as its head, and an office under him/her. It is a huge three storey building with amenities like separate canteen and cooks. All regular dishes are available in the hostel at a comparatively cheaper cost. Various indoor games are set inside the hostel to keep the inmates fresh and healthy. The service of a resident tutor is available to the hostellers round the clock.
