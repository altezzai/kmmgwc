Scholarship

The students of the college are conditionally eligible for one or more scholarships and freeships provided by various government departments at central and state levels. The majority of the students receive at least one scholarship apart from the E-grant facility of freeship available to the students belonging to families with an annual family income of less than Rs. 1 Lakh.
